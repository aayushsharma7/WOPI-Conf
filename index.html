About the Conference

This pioneering event aims to shine a light on the remarkable achievements of women in the field of optics and photonics across India and Asia. It also aims to explore the latest advancements, challenges, and future directions in the field of Optics and Photonics with a focus on emerging applications. It provides a platform to facilitate networking opportunities for professionals to connect with each other, as well as with industry leaders, researchers, and educators. We strive to showcase and acknowledge the achievements, innovations, and research contributions made by women in the Optics domain and to promote collaborations and partnerships that can enhance opportunities in the field.


Through various events and activities in this conference, following objectives will be addressed:

  • Foster Collaboration: Create a platform for networking, knowledge exchange, and collaboration among professionals in the field, breaking geographical and societal barriers.
  • Celebrate Achievements: Highlight the significant contributions of women in optics and photonics and their impact on advancing technologies and scientific understanding.
  • Promote Diversity: Emphasize the importance of gender diversity and inclusivity in the scientific community, and encourage more women to pursue careers in optics and photonics.
  • Inspire the Future: Empower the next generation of women scientists and engineers by showcasing role models and providing mentorship opportunities.

Concerning the WOPI series:

The inaugural edition of WOPI 2022, conducted at the Raman Research Institute in Bangalore, attracted over 100 participants, whereas the subsequent edition, WOPI 2023, hosted at IIT Madras, garnered an impressive turnout of over 150 participants from more than 30 institutions across India. The chosen contributions from WOPI 2023 were disseminated in the Journal of Physics-Photonics, Physics World, and Optics Communications. The third edition of WOPI 2024 took place at IIT Bombay, attracting over 200 participants from more than 40 institutions in India.

Our mission is to empower women scientists, researchers, and students in the field of optics and photonics by providing a platform for knowledge sharing, networking, and professional growth.

Registration includes full access to all conference sessions, workshops, and networking events over the three-day period. You'll also receive conference materials, access to the exhibition area, coffee breaks, and lunches each day. The welcome reception and networking dinners are also included in your registration.

Yes, we offer a special student rate of $199 for full-time students. You'll need to provide a valid student ID during registration. Additionally, we offer group discounts for 3 or more attendees from the same institution or organization. Please contact us at registration@womenoptics2025.com for more information about group rates.

We offer full refunds for cancellations made before August 1, 2025. Cancellations between August 1 and September 1 will receive a 50% refund. Unfortunately, no refunds are available for cancellations after September 1, 2025. However, registration can be transferred to another attendee at no additional cost by contacting our registration team.

The conference features a diverse range of sessions including keynote presentations from industry leaders, technical sessions covering the latest research, interactive workshops, panel discussions on career development and women in STEM, and poster sessions. We also have networking events and social activities throughout the three days.

Yes, we welcome abstract submissions for oral and poster presentations. The deadline for submissions is June 30, 2025. Visit our website's submission portal to submit your abstract. All submissions will be reviewed by our scientific committee, and authors will be notified of acceptance by July 15, 2025.

Yes, all keynote and technical sessions will be recorded and made available to registered attendees for up to 30 days after the conference. However, some workshops and interactive sessions may not be recorded due to their hands-on nature. Attendees will receive access information for the recordings after the conference.

The conference will take place at the San Francisco Convention Center, located at 747 Howard Street, San Francisco, CA 94103. The venue is centrally located in downtown San Francisco, easily accessible via public transportation and a short distance from many hotels and restaurants.

Yes, we have negotiated special rates at several hotels near the convention center. The Grand Hyatt San Francisco, Marriott Marquis, and Hotel Zetta are all within walking distance and offer conference rates. Book through our Venue page using code WOMENOPTICS2025 to receive the discounted rate. We recommend booking early as rooms will fill up quickly.

Taxis, ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft, and airport shuttles are also available. Travel time from SFO is typically 25-30 minutes, depending on traffic.
